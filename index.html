Breaking Communication Barriers for Students with Disabilities

Silent Speak is an innovative web application designed to help students with hearing, speech, color blindness, and dyslexia communicate effectively in classroom settings.

Key Features

Live Captioning

Real-time speech-to-text conversion for classroom lectures in multiple languages including Bangla, English, and Banglish.

Teacher-Student Sessions

Create virtual classrooms where teachers can share live captions and students can join with session codes.

Smart Notes

Save captions as notes with AI-powered formatting and organization for better study materials.

Pomodoro Timer

Focus timer with task management integrated with teacher-assigned homework and classwork.

Quick Communication

Pre-set buttons for common phrases and emergency communication with text-to-speech output.

Accessibility First

Color-blind friendly themes, adjustable font sizes, and customizable interface for diverse needs.

About Silent Speak

Silent Speak is an academic Design Project-2 initiative aimed at creating an inclusive communication platform for students with hearing, speech, color blindness, and dyslexia disabilities.

Our mission is to bridge the communication gap between teachers and students in classroom environments, ensuring every student has equal access to education regardless of their disabilities.

The platform uses cutting-edge web technologies including real-time speech recognition, AI-powered note formatting, and responsive design to work seamlessly on mobile devices.
