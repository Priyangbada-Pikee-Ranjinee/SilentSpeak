About Silent Speak

Silent Speak is a platform focused on creating an inclusive communication experience for students facing various challenges such as hearing, speech, color vision, and reading difficulties.

Our goal is to reduce communication gaps between teachers and students, making classroom interaction more accessible and effective for everyone.

The platform is built using modern web technologies to ensure smooth performance across devices, especially mobile, so students can use it anytime, anywhere.
