Terms and Conditions

Silent Speak Terms of Service

Welcome to Silent Speak, an academic project designed to help students with disabilities communicate effectively in classroom settings.

By using our service, you agree to:

  • Use the platform for educational purposes only
  • Respect the privacy and dignity of other users
  • Not share inappropriate content
  • Comply with your institution's code of conduct
  • Understand this is an academic project with limited support

This is a demonstration project for academic purposes. Real deployment would require additional legal agreements and privacy protections.

Privacy Policy

Silent Speak Privacy Policy

As an academic project, we take privacy seriously:

  • We collect minimal personal information needed for the service
  • Your disability information is used only to customize accessibility features
  • Classroom data (captions, messages) is stored temporarily for session functionality
  • We do not share your personal information with third parties
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time

This is a demonstration project. In a production environment, additional security measures would be implemented.
